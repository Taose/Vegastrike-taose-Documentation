const Point &  p3,
float  t

Hermite interpolate between p1 and p2. p0 and p3 are used for finding gradient at p1 and p2. this = p0 * (2t^2 - t^3 - t)/2

  • p1 * (3t^3 - 5t^2 + 2)/2
  • p2 * (4t^2 - 3t^3 + t)/2
  • p3 * (t^3 - t^2)/2
